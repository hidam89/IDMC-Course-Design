# Assessment Strategy

## Assessment Philosophy

Assessment in this course follows the principles of Outcome-Based Education (OBE) and Continuous Internal Evaluation (CIE). The objective is to evaluate students' conceptual understanding, analytical thinking, problem-solving ability, communication skills, and application of Informatica Intelligent Data Management Cloud (IDMC) concepts to enterprise scenarios.

The course adopts formative and summative assessment methods to encourage continuous learning and active participation.

# Assessment Description

## 1. Diagnostic Quiz

**Objective**

Assess students' prerequisite knowledge of:

- Cloud Computing
- Database Management Systems
- SQL
- Data Warehousing

---

## 2. Unit Quizzes

Short quizzes consisting of:

- Multiple Choice Questions
- True/False
- Fill in the Blanks
- Short Answer Questions

Purpose:

- Reinforce classroom learning
- Identify learning gaps
- Provide immediate feedback

---

## 3. Assignment

Students analyze enterprise scenarios involving Informatica Cloud services.

Example Topics

- Compare CDI and CAI for enterprise integration.
- Evaluate Cloud Data Quality in banking systems.
- Explain the importance of Master Data Management.

---

## 4. Case Study Discussion

Students work in groups to analyze real-world case studies.

Possible Case Studies

- Retail Customer 360
- Banking Data Quality
- Enterprise Data Integration
- Secure Agent Deployment Strategy

Students present:

- Business problem
- Proposed solution
- Expected outcomes
- Lessons learned

---

## 5. Group Presentation

Students present an enterprise solution using Informatica Cloud.

Presentation should include:

- Business scenario
- Selected Informatica services
- Proposed architecture
- Benefits
- Challenges

---

## 6. Reflection Journal

Students submit a short reflection covering:

- Key concepts learned
- Difficult concepts
- Industrial relevance
- Questions for further exploration

---

## 7. Mini Project

Students design (conceptually) an enterprise cloud data management solution.

Deliverables:

- Problem Statement
- Proposed Architecture
- Informatica Services Used
- Data Flow
- Expected Benefits
- Future Improvements

Programming is **not required**.

# Feedback Strategy

Feedback will be provided through:

- Quiz review sessions
- Assignment comments
- Case study discussions
- Presentation evaluation
- Individual consultation
- Reflection feedback

Students will be encouraged to improve based on constructive feedback.

---

# Academic Integrity

Students are expected to:

- Submit original work.
- Properly acknowledge all sources.
- Avoid plagiarism.
- Participate honestly in assessments.
- Follow university academic integrity policies.

Any violation will be handled according to university regulations.

---

# Assessment Outcomes

The assessment strategy enables students to:

- Demonstrate conceptual understanding.
- Apply enterprise cloud technologies.
- Analyze real-world business problems.
- Communicate technical solutions effectively.
- Develop ethical and professional practices.
