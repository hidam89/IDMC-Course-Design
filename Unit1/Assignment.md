# Assignment 1

## Title

Understanding Enterprise Data Management

### Type

Individual Assignment

### Course Outcome

CO1

### Bloom Level

L2 – Understand

### Marks

10

### Objective

Understand the importance of Enterprise Data Management in modern organizations.

### Task

Choose any organization such as:

- Amazon
- Flipkart
- SBI
- HDFC Bank
- Apollo Hospitals
- Your University

Identify:

- Five enterprise applications used
- Types of data generated
- Challenges in managing data
- Benefits of Enterprise Data Management

### Submission

2–3 page report with suitable diagrams.

# Assignment 2

## Title

Case Study: Selecting the Right IDMC Service

### Type

Individual Case Study

### Course Outcome

CO1

### Bloom Level

L3 – Apply

### Marks

10

### Scenario

A retail company wants to:

- Integrate customer data from CRM and ERP.
- Remove duplicate customer records.
- Maintain a single customer profile.
- Govern sensitive customer information.
- Share trusted datasets with analysts.

### Task

Identify:

1. Which IDMC service should be used for each requirement?
2. Explain why each service is appropriate.
3. Draw a simple architecture diagram.

### Deliverable

3–4 page report.
