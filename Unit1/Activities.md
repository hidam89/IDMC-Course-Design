# Activity 1

## Think–Pair–Share

### Topic

Enterprise Data Management

### Duration

10 Minutes

### Course Outcome

CO1

### Learning Objective

Students will understand why enterprise organizations require data management.

### Procedure

Step 1

Think individually.

Question

> How many software systems do you think Amazon uses?

Step 2

Discuss with partner.

Identify possible systems.

Examples

- CRM

- ERP

- Warehouse

- Delivery

- Payment

- Customer Support

Step 3

Share answers.

Faculty writes responses on board.

### Expected Learning

Students realize

Enterprise Data

is distributed

across multiple systems.
