# Classroom Discussion Guidelines

- Read the question carefully before answering.
- Support arguments with enterprise examples.
- Encourage multiple viewpoints.
- Respect differing opinions.
- Use diagrams where appropriate.
- Relate answers to real-world organizations whenever possible.

---

# Expected Learning Outcomes

After participating in these discussions, students will be able to:

- Analyze enterprise data management challenges.
- Evaluate different IDMC services for business scenarios.
- Design enterprise-level data management solutions.
- Defend technical decisions with logical reasoning.
- Collaborate effectively in technical discussions.
