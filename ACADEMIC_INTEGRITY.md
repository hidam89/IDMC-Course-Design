# Academic Integrity

Students are expected to uphold the highest standards of academic honesty.

## Students shall:

- Submit original work.
- Properly cite all sources.
- Avoid plagiarism.
- Respect intellectual property.
- Participate honestly in assessments.

---

## Appropriate Use of AI Tools

Students may use AI tools (such as ChatGPT or GitHub Copilot) for learning, brainstorming, and improving understanding.

However:

- AI-generated work must be reviewed and understood by the student.
- Final submissions must reflect the student's own understanding.
- Any significant AI assistance should be acknowledged if required by university policy.

---

## Professional Ethics

Students should:

- Respect peers.
- Work collaboratively.
- Maintain professionalism.
- Protect data privacy.
- Follow ethical practices in enterprise data management.
